Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect email addresses and usernames during registration. We do not collect financial data. Interaction logs (saved trends, chatbot usage) are stored to improve your experience.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide TrendZee services — personalized dashboards, saved trends, and AI-powered features. We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. AI & Third-Party Services

TrendZee uses Google Gemini API for AI-powered features. Questions submitted to the chatbot may be processed by Google's API. We only send trend-relevant context — never your personal information.

4. Data Security

Passwords are hashed using PBKDF2. CSRF protection is enabled on all forms. Sessions are secured with httpOnly cookies. Environment variables protect API keys.

5. Contact

Questions? Email privacy@trendzee.com
